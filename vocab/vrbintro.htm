Verbs - Introduction

This is going to be a very traditional presentation of verbs. I would love to do pictures to illustrate a verb, but there are three problems with that approach:

  1. A static picture can’t show action (which is what verbs are all about).
  2. I don’t have the artistic expertise to make QuickTime movies or animated GIF files to illustrate a verb; the QuickTime movies take too long to download anyway.
  3. Even if I could do the illustrations, how do you do a movie of the verb “to memorize”? How do you differentiate visually between “to learn” and “to study”?

Things could get very messy indeed. So it’s going to be just the verbs, presented in “dictionary form.”

Live with it.
