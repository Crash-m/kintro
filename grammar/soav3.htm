Subject-Object-Action Verb (part 3)

영수가   김치를   먹습니다.

This means “Yeong-su eats kimchi.”

죤이   책을   읽습니다.

This means either:

  • John reads a book.
  • John reads the book.

Korean doesn't have a word for definite or indefinite article; the context of the sentence makes the meaning clear.
